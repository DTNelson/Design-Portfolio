Paycrave Mobile App

Mobile App UX/UI Design

During my 2015 Bloc apprenticeship, I designed a mobile iOS 8 app called Paycrave, an all-in-one food truck locator, order, purchase, and rating app. The goals were to provide a comprehensive feature list, yet be delivered in a simple and intuitive manner. Bloc provided the basic functional requirements, user stories, style guide, and logo. I was tasked with developing the UX, UI, and IxD.

Personas

Novice vs Experienced App Users

I developed two personas for the Paycrave app. The first end-user, Mary, a retiree, is considered a novice user of mobile apps and only uses them when necessary. Her retiree friends recently started some local food truck businesses, and she wants to be able to support her friends. Mary wants to locate their trucks, order from her friends' trucks, then rate them to provide moral support.

Trey, an experienced mobile app user and software developer, wants to make fast, efficient, and healthier food choices to accommodate his busy schedule. To save time, Trey wants to be able to decide on a menu item and pay online before picking up his orders.

the design

Bloc.io provided the following user stories for the Paycrave mobile app:

  • As a user, I want to view food trucks near me on a map.
  • As a user, I want to select a truck, view details, and see a menu.
  • As a user, I want to create an account and view recent transactions.
  • As a user, I want to choose items to purchase and pay for them using my phone.
  • As a user, I want to rate and review a food truck, and view other ratings and reviews.

For a full demonstration of Paycrave's interaction design, see my Invision prototype.
